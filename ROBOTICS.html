Faiz and Miles Robotics Project

Maiz Bot: Autonomous Vacuum Bot

Our goal for this project is to create an autonomous vacuum that can clean in any environment it is placed in, similar and inspired by a Roomba. This entails the robot to be able to detect and avoid obstacles that it cannot clear, such as toys or certain furniture, and detect obstacles it can go under and clean, like table chairs and things of the like. Our robot will rely on 2D-LIDAR and SLAM to sense and create a map of its surroundings, as well as know its own location in a given room. In addition, we will have tactile sensors (bump sensor) in a situation where it bumps into an obstacle or wall and needs to pivot. It will use two wheels as its effectors, and have a vacuum box mounted under it to collect the debris it passes over. Lastly, the robot will move in a minesweeping manner where it will move from cell to cell until it reaches the other end of the room.
